Our People

Meet the Team

Behind Shree STC Group is a passionate team of professionals dedicated to delivering quality, trust, and excellence to every customer we serve.

Surendra Kumar Uprety

Managing Director / Chief Executive Officer

SHREE STC GROUP

With over 17 years of experience in the automobile parts industry, Surendra Kumar Uprety founded Shree STC Group with a vision to bring world-class automotive components to Nepal. His leadership and deep market knowledge have shaped the company into one of Nepal's most trusted auto parts distributors. He oversees all strategic partnerships, including the authorized distribution of CEAT Tyres and ENI Lubricants. He is also a key figure in the NADA Automobiles Association of Nepal, serving as its President.

Laxmi Uprety

Operations Manager / Managing Director

SHREE Kalsh Auto

Laxmi brings over a decade of expertise in supply chain and inventory management to Shree STC Group and Shree Kalsh Auto. He ensures seamless day-to-day operations from procurement and warehousing to timely delivery across Nepal. His meticulous approach to quality control and logistics has been instrumental in building the company's reputation for reliability and consistency. He also plays a pivotal role in fostering relationships with international suppliers, ensuring that the latest automotive technologies and products are available to the Nepalese market.

Inna Uprety Dharel

Operations Manager / Managing Director

STC Business link

Inna leads the sales and marketing division of STC Business link along with Shree STC group auto with energy and precision. She has been the driving force behind expanding STC's dealer and reseller network across Kathmandu and beyond. Her innovative marketing strategies and customer engagement initiatives have significantly increased brand visibility and customer loyalty. She is also a key liaison for international suppliers, ensuring that the latest automotive technologies and products are available to the Nepalese market.
